Practical Advice#

  1. To validate ACOPF formulations, compare ACPPowerFlow, ACRPowerFlow, and IVRPowerFlow on the same case.

  2. To validate convex relaxations, compare WSOCPowerFlow or BranchFlowSOCPowerFlow against PowerModels SOCWR/SOCBF-style models.

  3. For hybrid AC/DC cases, use PGLIB-HVDC/MatACDC data and set network_type = :acdc explicitly.
