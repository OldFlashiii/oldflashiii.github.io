Storage and Unit Commitment#

Storage and unit commitment are multi-period components. They usually require T > 1:

ctx = power_context(power; T = 24)
init_power!(ctx; formulation = :dcp, state_variables = :dc)
add_modules!(ctx, DCPPowerFlow(), GeneratorLimits(), StorageContinuous(), GenerationCost())

StorageContinuous#

Variables:

\[ p_{s,t}^{ch},p_{s,t}^{dis},e_{s,t} \]

Power bounds:

\[ 0\le p_{s,t}^{ch}\le \overline{P}_s^{ch},\qquad 0\le p_{s,t}^{dis}\le \overline{P}_s^{dis} \]

State-of-charge bounds:

\[ \underline{E}_s\le e_{s,t}\le \overline{E}_s \]

Energy recursion:

\[ e_{s,t}=e_{s,t-1} +\eta_s^{ch}p_{s,t}^{ch}\Delta t -\frac{1}{\eta_s^{dis}}p_{s,t}^{dis}\Delta t -\rho_s e_{s,t-1}\Delta t \]

Optional cyclic boundary:

\[ e_{s,0}=e_{s,T} \]

Network injection:

\[ P_{i,t}^{storage}=p_{s,t}^{dis}-p_{s,t}^{ch} \]

Class: LP by itself; the full model inherits the network formulation class.

StorageInteger and BESSMode1MIP#

Binary mode variables prevent simultaneous charge and discharge:

\[ u_{s,t}^{ch}+u_{s,t}^{dis}\le 1 \]
\[ 0\le p_{s,t}^{ch}\le \overline{P}_s^{ch}u_{s,t}^{ch} \]
\[ 0\le p_{s,t}^{dis}\le \overline{P}_s^{dis}u_{s,t}^{dis} \]

Class: MILP or MISOCP.

BESSInverterSOC#

Inverter capacity:

\[ (P_{s,t}^{ac})^2+(Q_{s,t}^{ac})^2\le (S_s^{inv})^2 \]

Reactive injection enters the AC bus balance:

\[ Q_{i,t}^{dev}\leftarrow Q_{i,t}^{dev}+Q_{s,t}^{ac} \]

Class: SOCP unless combined with a nonlinear AC formulation.

BESSNetPowerLP#

Net-power storage model:

\[ p_{s,t}^{net}=p_{s,t}^{dis}-p_{s,t}^{ch} \]
\[ -\overline{P}_s^{ch}\le p_{s,t}^{net}\le \overline{P}_s^{dis} \]

This is compact but does not enforce charge/discharge exclusivity by itself.

PumpedStorage2ModeMIP and PumpedStorage3StateConfig#

Two-mode pumped storage:

\[ u_{s,t}^{gen}+u_{s,t}^{pump}\le 1 \]
\[ 0\le p_{s,t}^{gen}\le \overline{P}_s^{gen}u_{s,t}^{gen} \]
\[ 0\le p_{s,t}^{pump}\le \overline{P}_s^{pump}u_{s,t}^{pump} \]

Reservoir recursion:

\[ e_{s,t}=e_{s,t-1} +\eta_s^{pump}p_{s,t}^{pump}\Delta t -\frac{1}{\eta_s^{gen}}p_{s,t}^{gen}\Delta t \]

Three-state model:

\[ u_{s,t}^{gen}+u_{s,t}^{pump}+u_{s,t}^{off}=1 \]

Class: MILP.

UnitCommitment1BinCA#

One-binary commitment:

\[ u_{g,t}\in\{0,1\} \]
\[ P_g^{min}u_{g,t}\le P_{g,t}\le P_g^{max}u_{g,t} \]

Startup and shutdown can be inferred:

\[ u_{g,t}-u_{g,t-1}\le y_{g,t},\qquad u_{g,t-1}-u_{g,t}\le z_{g,t} \]

UnitCommitment3BinStd#

Three-binary UC:

\[ u_{g,t},y_{g,t},z_{g,t}\in\{0,1\} \]
\[ u_{g,t}-u_{g,t-1}=y_{g,t}-z_{g,t} \]
\[ y_{g,t}+z_{g,t}\le 1 \]

Minimum up time:

\[ \sum_{\tau=t}^{t+T_g^{up}-1}u_{g,\tau}\ge T_g^{up}y_{g,t} \]

Minimum down time:

\[ \sum_{\tau=t}^{t+T_g^{down}-1}(1-u_{g,\tau})\ge T_g^{down}z_{g,t} \]

Class: MILP.

GeneratorRampLimits and tight ramping#

Basic ramping:

\[ P_{g,t}-P_{g,t-1}\le R_g^{up} \]
\[ P_{g,t-1}-P_{g,t}\le R_g^{down} \]

Startup/shutdown-aware ramping:

\[ P_{g,t}-P_{g,t-1} \le R_g^{up}u_{g,t-1}+P_g^{start}y_{g,t} \]
\[ P_{g,t-1}-P_{g,t} \le R_g^{down}u_{g,t}+P_g^{shut}z_{g,t} \]

StorageDegradationCost#

Throughput degradation:

\[ \min \sum_{s,t} c_s^{deg}\left(p_{s,t}^{ch}+p_{s,t}^{dis}\right)\Delta t \]

Piecewise degradation can use PWL convex combinations or segment variables.

References#

  • Storage formulations follow common multi-period OPF practice and are close to the PowerModels/PowerModelsDistribution storage model.

  • Tight unit commitment formulations: Morales-España, Latorre, and Ramos, 2013.

  • Pumped-storage and storage dispatch are standard time-coupled extensions of economic dispatch and unit commitment.
